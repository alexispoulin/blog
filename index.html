*LATEST NEWS* Home sweet home

After a blissful 3h of sleep and it was already time to wake up. Our adventure started with moving out of an appartment and it ended with a moving in another one. I was starting my course/class 4 days after we landed and we still needed to move in our new apartment. (My parents were complete savior and had visited and rented in our name an apartment for us). I don’t know if you ever tried to move after an 24h international flight and 3h of sleep but you need coffee and an awful lot of motivation. We were really happy to be home and be able to see our family.

What is bear and bee travel?
It’s a news and tips blog about our travels and hopefully it will help you plan your own trip! We decided to start traveling to escape the monotony of work, trafic, lunch, sleep, repeat. We wanted to go on a long trip to Asia for two years now, but we always pushed it back because of work opportunities or bad timing due to our lease or reason like lets accumulate more money, etc. We had so many good reason not to leave that we kept waiting. And then, sitting in the parking lot of a service Ontario on a saturday morning, we realised that we couldn’t keep pushing our dream to later for a life that we didn’t like. (the realisation came after two really crappy months, a funeral and an uncooperative government representative). So the next monday, we quit our jobs and 5 weeks later we were on a plane to Bangkok where our journey begins! If you wish to contact here you can send an email to BearAndBeeTravel2019@gmail.com

Ho Chi Minh

I treated myself with a new purse, the old one was in a pretty bad shape. Alexis also got a bag and we bought a vietnamiens tea set.
