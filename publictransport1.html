PUBLIC TRANSPORTATION


Public transportation

Bangkok
There are different options of public transportation in Bangkok. First one is of course the bus. We never took it because it doesn’t seem like easy option when you don’t speak thai… And also the bus seems really really crowded. But we use the BTS and the MRT a lot (Skytrain and Subway) they cover most of bangkok touristic area and are really easy to use (also cheap). In the train, you have announcement in english so you won’t have to worry about getting not off at the right station and for tickets they have ticket booth that are in english (or thai). The counter officier normally speaks a little english as well. And last but not least, the main advantage of the BTS and MRT system, it’s climatised !!
