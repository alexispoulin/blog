From Puerto Princesa to El Nido


Minibus with CURVES

Puerto Princesa to El nido by bus

The minivan
Like the names says it a minivan! There’s about 8-10 places in it and they will full it. It cost around 1300 PHP (35$ CAD). so about 17.5$ per person for the trip. The trip was around 5h but it may be longer (or shorter... ) depending on your driver. In our case, we had to hold on firmly to our seat in order to stay in place. Due to the many curves, we were sliding from one side to the other for the whole trip. Fortunately there’s A.C in the bus so we didn’t finished the transport like melted shaked cake.

The actual bus
**If you like your butt maybe don’t use that option**
This is like a normal city bus for 5 hours without A.C. But on a plus side it way cheaper than the minivans. It’s the option that most of the locals use to travel around the island. We still didn’t try it for the way back so I cannot comment on the quality of the driving on this method of transportation.
