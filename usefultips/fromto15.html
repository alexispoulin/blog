From Da Nang to Ho Chi Minh


Quick flight

Flight from Da Nang to Ho CHi ming

Booking
We booked to flight directly on the Jetstar website for 1518000 VND for the both of us (92$CAN). It was one direct flight to get there so a pretty easy transfer. Transfering from town to town in vietnam was easy their small airport almost everywhere and the flight are not too expensive. The only hiccup was that the flight left at 16h00 and that we lost the apartment at noon, so we had a bit of time to kill at the airport. After 1h30 hours of flight we landed in Ho Chi Minh safely.

The flight
The flight itself went very well with no problems whatsoever. The only thing is that we didn’t get the emergency seat. But we knew that, we previously flew with the Jetstar airline and they always charge for emergency seat unlike other airlines. But for an short flight it wasn’t really worth it.
