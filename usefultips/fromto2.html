From Bangkok to Phuket


Flying from Bangkok to Phuket

Booking
For this flight, we booked on a website call 12go.asia. It works very well for flight, bus and train ride in Asia. Like the name says, it's an Asian website so it regroup all local company. Most people book through that website. For us to go to Phuket, since it's a very popular tourist destination, we had choices of a few flight. Being the backpacker that we are, we choose the cheapest! The company was Airasia and It cost us 3336 baht (152.79$CAD) for the both of us with a 20 kg luggage. It left the Don Mueang airport around noon which was perfect for us!

Amenities on flight
None. It’s a 2h flight, you can always buy thing on board (bottle of water, some snacks) but that’s it.

Emergency seat
Again traveling with Alexis has some perk. We ask at the counter if the emergency seat where available and they gave them to us without any problem. (Some company charge for the emergency seat, but not airasia)
