From Phuket to Singapore


A flight with Jetstar Airline

Booking

For this flight, we found a cheaper price directly from google search engine. It cost us 152.79$CAD for 2 person.. it was a 3h flight.. pretty cheap. When you google Phuket to Singapore flight, google engine gives their own search engine for flight. Normally it’s higher than going on broker website, but not that time. Once we clicked on the google offer, it redirected us the Jetstar home website. The booking went well, although, it was the on-flight service that was a bit crappy. There was many empty seat with legroom, but we had to pay to access them, no free upgrade with Jetstar! But we didn’t expect much since the price was the lowest available. (all flights out of Phuket Island were a bit more expensive then the flight in)

Ameneties on fligth

There are no Amenities at all. You need to pay for everything and they will try to sell you things all flight long, teasing you with nice smell of freshly cooked food.

As we said before, Alexis is really tall and Jetstar have the least amount of legroom possible (I was squeezed in it too). It’s painful, but it’s their company policy does not to allow passengers to seat in emergency room unless they pay extra amount, no matter what. (Basically emergency exit are considered their first/business class seat)
