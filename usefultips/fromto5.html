Short ferry from Singapore

Booking
We booked the ticket online directly on the website of the company. Then printed the tickets. It was specified on the ticket to print it. The company we used was SindoFerry and it cost a total of 40$ (6$ per tickets, 14$ for the terminal taxes and 14$ for Surcharge. Everything was only mentioned when we committed to buy). There was a lot of different company doing the trip at different times. This company was leaving around noon so it was perfect for us.
